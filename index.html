About Me

Hello! My name is Tatiana and I am an aspiring full-stack developer. Currently, I am a Junior at Michigan State University pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science and Information Science through the Honors College.

Beyond my classes, I am very passionate about outreach and increasing the diversity within the profession. Starting my first year at MSU, I have been involved in the MSU Coding Clubs, which teach programming to children in grades 6-12. Since beginning, I have been involved in most aspects of creating these clubs: planning, teaching, mentoring, and more.

In addition, I have been a part of the Women In Computing club since my start at MSU. Through this club, I have been able to make several lasting connections, as well as having the opportunity to attend the Grace Hopper Conference in September 2022.

Outside of college, I have had one software engineering internship over Summer 2022, where I learned a lot about what a professional software engineering role may look like. I was taught how to use JIRA and Crucible in order to use it on a daily basis throughout the internship. Additionally, I learned more about Java and MongoDB, both of which were languages used in my project.
